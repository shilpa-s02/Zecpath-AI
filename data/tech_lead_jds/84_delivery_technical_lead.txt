84. Delivery Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Delivery Technical Lead ensures successful end-to-end technical delivery across 
projects, balancing quality, timelines, and customer expectations. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead technical delivery across multiple teams or projects 
• Align architecture and development with delivery commitments 
• Monitor progress, quality metrics, and risks 
• Coordinate with delivery managers and PMOs 
• Drive continuous improvement in delivery processes 
• Handle escalation of complex technical issues 
Required Skills 
• Multi-project delivery management 
• Technical architecture understanding 
• Process optimization 
• Risk and dependency management 
• Strong leadership and communication 
Experience 
• 8–14 years in engineering 
• 4+ years in delivery or lead roles
