66. GRC Technical Lead (Governance, Risk & 
Compliance) 
Role Overview 
The GRC Technical Lead ensures security governance, risk management, and regulatory 
compliance across technology systems. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define and enforce security governance frameworks 
• Conduct risk assessments and compliance audits 
• Map technical controls to regulatory requirements 
• Collaborate with legal, audit, and IT teams 
• Manage compliance reporting and remediation 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• GRC frameworks (ISO 27001, NIST, SOC 2) 
• Risk assessment and compliance tools 
• Regulatory standards (GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS) 
• Policy and control management 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–15 years of GRC experience 
• Experience in regulated industries
