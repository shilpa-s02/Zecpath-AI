46. Big Data Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Big Data Technical Lead leads high-volume, high-velocity data processing systems for 
large-scale analytics and real-time insights. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and optimize big data processing frameworks 
• Lead distributed data processing and storage solutions 
• Ensure fault tolerance and scalability 
• Manage performance tuning and cost optimization 
• Guide teams on best practices for big data ecosystems 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Hadoop ecosystem, Spark, Hive 
• Distributed storage systems 
• Streaming and batch processing frameworks 
• Cloud-based big data platforms 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–15 years of big data experience 
• Expertise in large-scale distributed systems
