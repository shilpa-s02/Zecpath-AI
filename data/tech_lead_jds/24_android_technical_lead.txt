24. Android Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Android Technical Lead leads development of native Android applications with focus on 
performance, stability, and modern Android architecture. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Architect Android applications using modern frameworks 
• Guide teams on Kotlin, Jetpack components 
• Ensure best practices in app performance and security 
• Review code and mentor developers 
• Collaborate with backend and product teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Kotlin, Java 
• Android SDK, Jetpack 
• MVVM, Clean Architecture 
• CI/CD and Play Store deployment 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 6–11 years of Android development experience 
• Experience leading Android app teams
