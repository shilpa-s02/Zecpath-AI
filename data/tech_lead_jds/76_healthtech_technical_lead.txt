76. HealthTech Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The HealthTech Technical Lead is responsible for building compliant, secure, and scalable 
healthcare platforms such as EHR, telemedicine, and health analytics systems. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead architecture of healthcare applications 
• Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations 
• Integrate medical devices and health data platforms 
• Oversee data security, privacy, and interoperability 
• Collaborate with clinicians, product, and compliance teams 
Required Skills 
• Healthcare systems (EHR, EMR, HIS) 
• HL7, FHIR standards 
• Java / Python / .NET 
• Cloud security and encryption 
• HIPAA, GDPR compliance 
Experience 
• 7–10 years in healthcare IT 
• 3+ years in technical leadership roles
