25. iOS Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The iOS Technical Lead is responsible for leading development of high-quality iOS 
applications using Apple’s ecosystem and best practices. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Architect iOS applications using Swift 
• Guide teams on UIKit / SwiftUI 
• Ensure app performance, security, and stability 
• Lead App Store submission and release processes 
• Mentor iOS engineers 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Swift, Objective-C (optional) 
• iOS SDK, SwiftUI 
• REST APIs, mobile security 
• CI/CD for iOS 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 6–11 years of iOS development experience 
• Leadership in consumer or enterprise iOS apps
