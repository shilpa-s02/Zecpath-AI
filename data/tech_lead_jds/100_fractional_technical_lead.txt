100. Fractional Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Fractional Technical Lead provides part-time or on-demand technical leadership, 
typically for startups or growing organizations that need senior expertise without full-time 
commitment. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define technical direction and architecture 
• Mentor in-house engineering teams 
• Review code, systems, and technical decisions 
• Support hiring and team scaling 
• Guide technology roadmap and priorities 
• Provide executive-level technical advice 
Required Skills 
• Broad technical and architectural expertise 
• Startup and scaling experience 
• Strategic and hands-on leadership 
• Strong communication with founders and executives 
• Flexible and outcome-driven mindset 
Engagement Type 
• Part-time / Advisory / Retainer-based 
Experience 
• 12–20 years in engineering 
• Prior senior leadership or architect roles
