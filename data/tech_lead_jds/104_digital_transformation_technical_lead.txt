104. Digital Transformation Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Digital Transformation Technical Lead drives modernization initiatives, enabling 
organizations to adopt cloud, AI, automation, and agile delivery models. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead modernization and transformation programs 
• Migrate legacy systems to modern architectures 
• Enable cloud-native and AI-driven solutions 
• Align technology with business transformation goals 
• Drive DevOps, automation, and agile adoption 
• Measure and report transformation outcomes 
Required Skills 
• Legacy modernization 
• Cloud and platform engineering 
• Automation and AI adoption 
• Enterprise architecture 
• Change and transformation leadership 
Experience 
• 10–18 years in engineering 
• 5+ years in transformation programs
