85. Client Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Client Technical Lead serves as the primary technical point of contact for clients, 
ensuring solutions meet business and technical expectations. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Act as technical liaison between client and internal teams 
• Understand client requirements and propose technical solutions 
• Lead technical discussions, demos, and reviews 
• Ensure alignment between delivery and client expectations 
• Manage change requests and technical escalations 
• Support pre-sales and solution design activities 
Required Skills 
• Client-facing communication 
• Solution architecture 
• Technical documentation 
• Presentation and negotiation skills 
• Strong domain knowledge 
Experience 
• 7–12 years in software development 
• 3+ years in client-facing technical roles
