15. Golang Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Golang Technical Lead designs and leads high-performance, concurrent, and cloud
native systems using Go. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Architect backend services using Go 
• Design microservices and distributed systems 
• Ensure high concurrency, reliability, and performance 
• Lead code reviews and enforce Go best practices 
• Collaborate with DevOps and SRE teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Golang, goroutines, concurrency patterns 
• gRPC, REST APIs 
• Kubernetes, Docker 
• Distributed systems and observability tools 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 6–10 years of backend engineering experience 
• Strong hands-on experience with Golang in production
