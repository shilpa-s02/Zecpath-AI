72. Dynamics 365 Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Dynamics 365 Technical Lead is responsible for leading implementation, customization, 
and integration of Microsoft Dynamics 365 solutions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design Dynamics 365 architecture and custom solutions 
• Develop plugins, workflows, and Power Platform solutions 
• Manage integrations using Azure services 
• Ensure performance, security, and scalability 
• Guide Dynamics developers and consultants 
Required Skills 
• Dynamics 365 CE / F&O 
• C#, .NET, Power Platform 
• Azure Logic Apps, Functions 
• REST APIs 
• Agile methodologies 
Experience 
• 6–10 years with Microsoft ecosystem 
• 3+ years in technical leadership
