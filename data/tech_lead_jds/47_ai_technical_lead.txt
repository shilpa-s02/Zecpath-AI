47. AI Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The AI Technical Lead drives development and deployment of AI-powered systems, ensuring 
ethical, scalable, and business-aligned AI solutions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead AI solution design and model integration 
• Collaborate with data scientists and engineers 
• Ensure responsible AI, fairness, and governance 
• Translate business problems into AI use cases 
• Oversee model deployment and lifecycle management 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• AI/ML fundamentals and frameworks 
• Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch 
• Data pipelines and cloud AI services 
• AI ethics and governance practices 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–14 years of experience in AI/ML systems 
• Leadership in AI product or platform delivery
