59. Chaos Engineering Lead 
Role Overview 
The Chaos Engineering Lead improves system resilience by proactively testing systems 
under failure conditions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and execute chaos experiments 
• Identify system weaknesses and failure points 
• Collaborate with SRE and DevOps teams 
• Improve system reliability and fault tolerance 
• Establish chaos engineering practices and tooling 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Chaos engineering tools (Chaos Monkey, Gremlin) 
• Distributed systems and cloud platforms 
• Observability and monitoring tools 
• Incident management practices 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–14 years of experience 
• Background in SRE, DevOps, or reliability engineering
