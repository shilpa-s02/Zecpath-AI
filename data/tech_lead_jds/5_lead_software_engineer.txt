5. Lead Software Engineer 
Role Overview 
The Lead Software Engineer is a senior hands-on role combining advanced coding expertise 
with technical leadership responsibilities. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and develop complex software components 
• Lead technical discussions and design reviews 
• Mentor junior and mid-level engineers 
• Ensure performance optimization and code quality 
• Collaborate closely with architects and product teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Advanced programming and software design skills 
• Strong knowledge of data structures and algorithms 
• Experience with scalable application development 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–11 years of software engineering experience 
• Proven leadership in complex software projects
