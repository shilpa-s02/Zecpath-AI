96. Freelance Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Freelance Technical Lead provides short- to medium-term technical leadership for 
projects, startups, or client engagements, delivering architecture, execution guidance, and 
team mentorship. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead system design and technical execution for assigned projects 
• Review code, architecture, and technical decisions 
• Guide teams on best practices and delivery standards 
• Resolve complex technical challenges 
• Collaborate with product owners and stakeholders 
• Deliver outcomes within agreed timelines and scope 
Required Skills 
• Strong hands-on engineering and architecture 
• Ability to quickly understand existing systems 
• Independent decision-making 
• Excellent communication and documentation 
• Agile and remote collaboration skills 
Engagement Type 
• Contract / Project-based / Remote 
Experience 
• 8–15 years in software engineering 
• Proven technical leadership across multiple projects
