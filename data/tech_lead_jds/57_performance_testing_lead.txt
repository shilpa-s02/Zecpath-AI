57. Performance Testing Lead 
Role Overview 
The Performance Testing Lead ensures applications meet performance, scalability, and 
reliability requirements under real-world load. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define performance testing strategy and benchmarks 
• Design load, stress, and endurance tests 
• Analyze performance bottlenecks and system behavior 
• Collaborate with developers and infrastructure teams 
• Provide performance recommendations and reports 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• JMeter, LoadRunner, Gatling 
• Performance monitoring and APM tools 
• System and network performance analysis 
• Capacity planning 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–13 years of performance testing experience 
• Expertise in large-scale systems
