87. Offshore Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Offshore Technical Lead leads offshore engineering teams, ensuring productivity, 
quality, and alignment with onsite and client expectations. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead offshore technical teams 
• Translate client requirements into technical tasks 
• Ensure code quality and delivery timelines 
• Coordinate with onsite leads and delivery managers 
• Mentor offshore engineers 
• Manage dependencies and technical risks 
Required Skills 
• Distributed team leadership 
• Strong hands-on technical skills 
• Agile development practices 
• Communication across time zones 
• Quality assurance and review processes 
Experience 
• 7–12 years in software development 
• 3+ years leading offshore teams
