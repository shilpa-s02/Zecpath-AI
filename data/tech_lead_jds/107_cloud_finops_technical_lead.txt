107. Cloud FinOps Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Cloud FinOps Technical Lead optimizes cloud cost, performance, and value through 
engineering-driven financial accountability. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead cloud cost optimization initiatives 
• Design cost-efficient architectures 
• Implement FinOps tooling and metrics 
• Collaborate with finance, engineering, and product teams 
• Monitor usage, budgets, and forecasts 
• Drive a culture of cost ownership 
Required Skills 
• Cloud cost management and FinOps practices 
• Cloud architecture and optimization 
• Monitoring and cost analytics tools 
• Financial modeling basics 
• Stakeholder communication 
Experience 
• 8–14 years in cloud engineering 
• 3+ years in FinOps or cost optimization roles
