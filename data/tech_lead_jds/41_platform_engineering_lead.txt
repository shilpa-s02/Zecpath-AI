41. Platform Engineering Lead 
Role Overview 
The Platform Engineering Lead builds and operates internal platforms that enable developers 
to ship software efficiently and reliably. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design internal developer platforms and tooling 
• Enable self-service infrastructure and CI/CD 
• Standardize observability, security, and compliance 
• Collaborate with SRE and product teams 
• Drive platform scalability and reliability 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Kubernetes, cloud platforms 
• Infrastructure automation and IaC 
• Observability and platform security 
• Developer experience (DevEx) tools 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–14 years of experience 
• Platform or SRE background
