54. QA Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The QA Technical Lead oversees quality assurance strategy, test execution, and continuous 
quality improvement across applications and platforms. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define QA strategy, test plans, and quality standards 
• Lead manual and automated testing efforts 
• Coordinate QA activities across teams and releases 
• Ensure defect tracking, reporting, and resolution 
• Collaborate with development, product, and DevOps teams 
• Mentor QA engineers and testers 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Manual and automated testing methodologies 
• Test management tools (JIRA, TestRail) 
• SDLC and Agile/Scrum processes 
• CI/CD quality integration 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–12 years of QA experience 
• Prior experience in QA leadership roles
