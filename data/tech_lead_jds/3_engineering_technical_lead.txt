3. Engineering Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Engineering Technical Lead provides technical leadership across engineering teams, 
focusing on scalable architecture, innovation, and engineering excellence. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define and enforce engineering best practices 
• Guide teams on system scalability and performance 
• Review and approve technical designs and architecture 
• Support engineering managers in delivery planning 
• Resolve high-impact technical risks 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Strong engineering fundamentals and architecture expertise 
• Experience with distributed systems and microservices 
• Cloud infrastructure and observability knowledge 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–14 years of engineering experience 
• Experience leading cross-functional engineering teams
