101. Autonomous Systems Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Autonomous Systems Technical Lead leads design and deployment of self-managing, 
self-healing, and decision-making systems powered by AI, automation, and real-time data. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Architect autonomous and self-adaptive systems 
• Lead development of event-driven and feedback-loop architectures 
• Implement self-healing, auto-scaling, and autonomous decision engines 
• Integrate AI models with operational systems 
• Ensure safety, reliability, and observability of autonomous behavior 
• Collaborate with AI, SRE, and platform teams 
Required Skills 
• Distributed and autonomous systems 
• AI/ML integration 
• Event-driven architecture 
• Observability and control loops 
• Cloud-native platforms (Kubernetes, serverless) 
Experience 
• 10–16 years in systems engineering 
• 4+ years in AI-driven or autonomous platforms
