33. Integration Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Integration Technical Lead is responsible for designing and managing system 
integrations across internal and external platforms. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design integration architectures and data exchange models 
• Lead API, event-driven, and middleware integrations 
• Ensure data consistency, security, and reliability 
• Troubleshoot complex integration issues 
• Coordinate with third-party vendors and partners 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• API management platforms 
• Messaging systems (Kafka, RabbitMQ) 
• ESB, iPaaS, and middleware solutions 
• Security and data governance 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–14 years of integration experience 
• Leadership in enterprise integration projects
