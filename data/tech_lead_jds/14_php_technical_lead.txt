14. PHP Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The PHP Technical Lead oversees development of web and backend systems using PHP, 
ensuring maintainability, performance, and secure coding practices. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead PHP application development and architecture 
• Guide teams on Laravel, Symfony, or CodeIgniter 
• Review code and ensure best practices 
• Optimize application performance and scalability 
• Coordinate with frontend, QA, and DevOps teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• PHP 7/8, Laravel/Symfony 
• RESTful APIs, MVC architecture 
• MySQL/PostgreSQL 
• CI/CD and cloud hosting platforms 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 6–11 years of PHP development experience 
• Experience leading web application teams
