39. GCP Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The GCP Technical Lead designs and leads cloud solutions on Google Cloud Platform, 
focusing on data-driven and cloud-native architectures. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design architectures using GCP services 
• Lead teams on GKE, Compute Engine, Cloud Run 
• Implement security and IAM best practices 
• Optimize performance and cloud costs 
• Collaborate with data and ML teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• GCP services and networking 
• Kubernetes and container platforms 
• Terraform and CI/CD tools 
• Cloud monitoring and logging 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–12 years of experience 
• GCP certifications (preferred)
