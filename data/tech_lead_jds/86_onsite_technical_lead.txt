86. Onsite Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Onsite Technical Lead works directly at the client location to oversee technical 
execution, coordination, and communication between onsite and offshore teams. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Represent technical leadership at client site 
• Coordinate requirements, clarifications, and feedback 
• Lead onsite technical discussions and planning 
• Ensure smooth collaboration with offshore teams 
• Track progress and report status to stakeholders 
• Resolve onsite technical challenges quickly 
Required Skills 
• Strong communication and interpersonal skills 
• Technical leadership and problem-solving 
• Cross-cultural collaboration 
• Agile delivery experience 
• Stakeholder management 
Experience 
• 8–14 years in engineering 
• 4+ years in onsite or client-facing roles
