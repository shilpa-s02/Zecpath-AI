1. Technical Lead (Tech Lead) 
Role Overview 
The Technical Lead is responsible for leading the technical direction of projects, mentoring 
developers, ensuring code quality, and translating business requirements into scalable 
technical solutions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead system design, architecture decisions, and technical planning 
• Review code, enforce best practices, and maintain high engineering standards 
• Mentor and guide development team members 
• Collaborate with product managers, architects, and stakeholders 
• Troubleshoot complex technical issues and ensure timely delivery 
• Ensure performance, scalability, security, and reliability of systems 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Strong expertise in one or more programming languages 
• Solid understanding of system design, databases, and APIs 
• Experience with cloud platforms and DevOps practices 
• Knowledge of Agile/Scrum methodologies 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 6–10 years of software development experience 
• Prior experience leading small to mid-sized teams
