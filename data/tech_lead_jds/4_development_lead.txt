4. Development Lead 
Role Overview 
The Development Lead manages daily development activities, ensures sprint commitments, 
and provides hands-on technical guidance to developers. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Plan and execute development sprints and releases 
• Assign tasks and monitor progress 
• Review code and ensure adherence to standards 
• Collaborate with product owners and QA teams 
• Support issue resolution and production fixes 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Strong coding skills and debugging expertise 
• Agile/Scrum delivery experience 
• Familiarity with CI/CD and version control systems 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 6–10 years of development experience 
• Prior experience as a senior developer or module lead
