80. Gaming Platform Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Gaming Platform Technical Lead leads development of scalable, real-time gaming 
platforms supporting multiplayer, matchmaking, and monetization systems. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Architect high-performance gaming backends 
• Optimize low-latency real-time systems 
• Integrate game engines and backend services 
• Implement player data, leaderboards, and analytics 
• Ensure scalability for global traffic 
Required Skills 
• Real-time systems & multiplayer architecture 
• C++ / C# / Node.js 
• Game engines (Unity, Unreal) 
• WebSockets, gRPC 
• Cloud scaling & performance tuning 
Experience 
• 7–12 years in gaming or real-time systems 
• 3+ years in platform leadership
