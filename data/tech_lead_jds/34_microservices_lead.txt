34. Microservices Lead 
Role Overview 
The Microservices Lead owns the design, implementation, and governance of microservices
based architectures across the organization. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define microservices architecture and service boundaries 
• Guide teams on service communication and data ownership 
• Implement resilience, observability, and fault tolerance 
• Ensure secure and scalable microservices deployment 
• Manage service versioning and lifecycle 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Microservices architecture patterns 
• Kubernetes, Docker, and cloud platforms 
• Service meshes and observability tools 
• Distributed tracing and monitoring 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–13 years of backend or platform experience 
• Deep hands-on experience with microservices at scale
