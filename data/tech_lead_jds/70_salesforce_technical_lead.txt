70. Salesforce Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Salesforce Technical Lead drives design, development, and customization of Salesforce 
solutions aligned with business goals. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead Salesforce architecture and development 
• Customize Salesforce using Apex, LWC, and Flows 
• Integrate Salesforce with external systems 
• Ensure security, scalability, and performance 
• Support DevOps pipelines and CI/CD 
• Mentor Salesforce developers 
Required Skills 
• Salesforce Apex, LWC, Visualforce 
• Salesforce APIs & integrations 
• Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Experience Cloud 
• Salesforce security model 
• Agile/Scrum 
Experience 
• 6–10 years in Salesforce development 
• 3+ years in leadership roles
