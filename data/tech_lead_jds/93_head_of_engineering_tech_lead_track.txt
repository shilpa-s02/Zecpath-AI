93. Head of Engineering (Tech Lead Track) 
Role Overview 
The Head of Engineering (Tech Lead Track) leads engineering execution while remaining 
deeply involved in technical decisions and architecture. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead engineering teams and technical direction 
• Own architecture, code quality, and engineering standards 
• Partner with product and business leaders 
• Scale teams, systems, and processes 
• Mentor engineering managers and senior engineers 
• Drive delivery excellence and innovation 
Required Skills 
• Strong hands-on technical background 
• Engineering leadership and people management 
• Architecture and system design 
• Agile scaling frameworks 
• Strategic planning and execution 
Experience 
• 14–20 years in engineering 
• 6+ years in senior technical leadership roles
