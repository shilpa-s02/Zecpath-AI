8. Lead Platform Engineer 
Role Overview 
The Lead Platform Engineer builds and maintains internal platforms that enable scalable, 
secure, and efficient product development. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and lead internal developer platforms 
• Build reusable infrastructure and platform services 
• Enable CI/CD, observability, and self-service tooling 
• Drive platform reliability and performance 
• Collaborate with product and SRE teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Kubernetes, cloud platforms, and automation tools 
• Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, CloudFormation) 
• Platform observability and security best practices 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–13 years of engineering experience 
• Strong background in DevOps or SRE practices
