53. NLP Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The NLP Technical Lead designs and deploys natural language processing systems for 
search, chatbots, and language understanding. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Architect NLP pipelines and language models 
• Lead model training, fine-tuning, and evaluation 
• Implement text analytics, search, and conversational AI 
• Ensure model performance and fairness 
• Collaborate with GenAI and product teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• NLP techniques and language models 
• Transformers, LLMs 
• Python, NLP libraries 
• Model evaluation and deployment tools 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–13 years of NLP experience 
• Leadership in enterprise NLP solutions
