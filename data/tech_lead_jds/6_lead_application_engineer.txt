6. Lead Application Engineer 
Role Overview 
The Lead Application Engineer focuses on designing, building, and maintaining enterprise
grade applications while guiding application development teams. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead application architecture and design decisions 
• Ensure application scalability, security, and performance 
• Manage application lifecycle from development to production 
• Guide developers on application frameworks and best practices 
• Coordinate with infrastructure and DevOps teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Strong experience in application frameworks and APIs 
• Database design and performance tuning 
• Cloud deployment and monitoring tools 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–12 years of application development experience 
• Experience leading enterprise or SaaS applications
