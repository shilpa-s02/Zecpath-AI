89. Senior Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Senior Technical Lead owns complex technical initiatives, mentors multiple teams, and 
ensures architecture, quality, and delivery align with business objectives. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead design and development of complex systems 
• Guide and mentor Technical Leads and Senior Engineers 
• Review architecture decisions and critical code paths 
• Drive engineering best practices and standards 
• Collaborate with product, delivery, and architecture teams 
• Resolve high-impact technical issues and escalations 
Required Skills 
• Advanced system design and architecture 
• Deep expertise in one or more tech stacks 
• Mentoring and leadership skills 
• Agile delivery and DevOps practices 
• Strong stakeholder communication 
Experience 
• 10–14 years in software engineering 
• 4+ years in technical leadership roles
