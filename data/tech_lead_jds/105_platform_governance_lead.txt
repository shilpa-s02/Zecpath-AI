105. Platform Governance Lead 
Role Overview 
The Platform Governance Lead ensures consistency, security, compliance, and scalability 
across shared technology platforms. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define platform governance frameworks 
• Establish standards, policies, and controls 
• Monitor compliance and platform usage 
• Balance developer autonomy with governance 
• Collaborate with security, compliance, and platform teams 
• Drive continuous governance improvement 
Required Skills 
• Platform engineering 
• Governance and compliance frameworks 
• Security and risk management 
• Metrics and reporting 
• Cross-team influence 
Experience 
• 9–15 years in platform or enterprise systems 
• 4+ years in governance or lead roles
