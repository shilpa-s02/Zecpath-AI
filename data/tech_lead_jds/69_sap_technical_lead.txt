69. SAP Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The SAP Technical Lead leads SAP system development, customization, and integration 
across modules while ensuring performance, compliance, and scalability. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design SAP technical architecture (ECC / S/4HANA) 
• Lead ABAP, Fiori, BTP, and integration development 
• Manage SAP enhancements, workflows, and reports 
• Coordinate with SAP functional leads (FI, CO, MM, SD, HCM) 
• Ensure system security and compliance 
• Support SAP upgrades and migrations 
Required Skills 
• SAP ABAP, CDS Views, OData 
• SAP Fiori/UI5 
• SAP BTP, PI/PO, CPI 
• S/4HANA migration experience 
• Strong debugging and performance tuning skills 
Experience 
• 8–15 years in SAP environments 
• 4+ years as SAP Technical Lead
