32. Software Architecture Lead 
Role Overview 
The Software Architecture Lead provides strategic oversight of software design, ensuring 
quality, extensibility, and long-term maintainability across products. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define software architecture vision and standards 
• Guide teams on object-oriented and service-oriented design 
• Ensure consistency across software components 
• Evaluate new technologies and architectural approaches 
• Lead architecture governance and reviews 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Software design principles and patterns 
• Microservices and monolithic architectures 
• DevOps and CI/CD alignment 
• Cross-platform software development 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 10–16 years of software engineering experience 
• Strong background in architecture leadership
