60. Quality Engineering Lead 
Role Overview 
The Quality Engineering Lead embeds quality into the entire SDLC, focusing on automation, 
reliability, and engineering excellence. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define quality engineering vision and standards 
• Promote shift-left and continuous testing practices 
• Drive automation-first quality strategies 
• Collaborate with development and platform teams 
• Measure and improve quality KPIs 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Quality engineering and automation frameworks 
• CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices 
• Agile and continuous delivery 
• Metrics-driven quality improvement 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–14 years of experience 
• Leadership in quality transformation initiatives
