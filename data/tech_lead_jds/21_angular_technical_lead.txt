21. Angular Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Angular Technical Lead is responsible for leading enterprise-scale Angular applications, 
focusing on modularity, performance, and maintainability. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead Angular application architecture 
• Guide teams on RxJS, NgRx, and Angular best practices 
• Ensure secure and scalable frontend solutions 
• Review code and mentor developers 
• Collaborate with backend and DevOps teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Angular, TypeScript 
• RxJS, NgRx 
• Angular Material 
• CI/CD and testing frameworks 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 6–11 years of frontend experience 
• Enterprise Angular project leadership
