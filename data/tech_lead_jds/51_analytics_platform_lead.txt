51. Analytics Platform Lead 
Role Overview 
The Analytics Platform Lead owns analytics infrastructure that enables BI, reporting, and 
self-service analytics across the organization. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and manage analytics platforms and tools 
• Enable self-service dashboards and reporting 
• Ensure data governance, security, and access control 
• Optimize performance and scalability 
• Collaborate with business and analytics teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• BI tools (Power BI, Tableau, Looker) 
• Data modeling and semantic layers 
• SQL and data warehousing 
• Analytics governance frameworks 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–13 years of analytics platform experience 
• Leadership in enterprise analytics environments
