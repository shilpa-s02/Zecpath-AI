95. Director of Engineering (Hands-on Track) 
Role Overview 
The Director of Engineering (Hands-on Track) leads multiple engineering teams while 
remaining involved in architecture, design, and critical technical decisions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead engineering managers and technical leads 
• Oversee delivery, architecture, and technical quality 
• Guide long-term platform and system evolution 
• Balance execution speed with technical excellence 
• Partner with executives on product and technology strategy 
• Drive talent growth and engineering culture 
Required Skills 
• Large-scale system architecture 
• Engineering management and leadership 
• Cross-functional collaboration 
• Metrics-driven delivery management 
• Strong technical judgment 
Experience 
• 15–22 years in engineering 
• 6+ years leading large engineering organizations 
Consulting, Freelance & Contract Technical Leads
