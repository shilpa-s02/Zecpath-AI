103. Low-Code / No-Code Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Low-Code / No-Code Technical Lead governs and scales enterprise low-code platforms 
while ensuring security, quality, and maintainability. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead low-code/no-code platform strategy 
• Define governance, standards, and guardrails 
• Integrate low-code solutions with core systems 
• Enable citizen developers while managing risk 
• Monitor performance, security, and scalability 
• Mentor teams on platform best practices 
Required Skills 
• Low-code platforms (Power Platform, Mendix, OutSystems) 
• Integration and API design 
• Security and governance frameworks 
• Platform enablement 
• Change management 
Experience 
• 8–12 years in enterprise systems 
• 3+ years in low-code platform leadership
