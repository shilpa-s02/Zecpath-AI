9. Lead Product Engineer 
Role Overview 
The Lead Product Engineer bridges product vision and engineering execution, ensuring 
customer-focused, high-quality product development. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Translate product requirements into technical solutions 
• Lead feature design and implementation 
• Collaborate closely with product managers and UX teams 
• Ensure rapid iteration and continuous improvement 
• Balance technical excellence with time-to-market 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Full-stack development expertise 
• Strong understanding of product lifecycle and UX 
• Agile product development experience 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–12 years of product-focused engineering experience 
• Experience leading customer-facing products
