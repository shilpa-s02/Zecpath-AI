30. Solution Design Lead 
Role Overview 
The Solution Design Lead translates business requirements into end-to-end technical 
solutions, ensuring feasibility, scalability, and alignment with enterprise architecture. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Analyze business and functional requirements 
• Design complete technical solutions across systems 
• Create solution architecture diagrams and documentation 
• Coordinate with enterprise architects and delivery teams 
• Evaluate risks, trade-offs, and cost implications 
• Support pre-sales, RFPs, and client discussions 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Solution architecture and system integration 
• Cloud platforms and enterprise systems 
• API-led connectivity and integration patterns 
• Strong stakeholder communication skills 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 9–15 years of IT experience 
• Experience in solution architecture or consulting roles
