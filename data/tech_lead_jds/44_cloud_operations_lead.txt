44. Cloud Operations Lead 
Role Overview 
The Cloud Operations Lead manages day-to-day cloud operations, ensuring uptime, cost 
efficiency, and operational excellence. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Oversee cloud operations and support teams 
• Monitor system health and availability 
• Manage incidents, escalations, and SLAs 
• Optimize cloud costs and usage 
• Enforce operational and security standards 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Cloud platforms and operations tooling 
• ITIL and operational best practices 
• Monitoring, logging, and cost tools 
• Incident and problem management 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–13 years of operations experience 
• Experience managing cloud production systems
