45. Data Engineering Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Data Engineering Technical Lead is responsible for designing, building, and scaling 
reliable data pipelines and platforms that power analytics, AI, and business intelligence. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Architect and lead end-to-end data pipelines (batch & streaming) 
• Design scalable data lakes and data warehouses 
• Ensure data quality, governance, and security standards 
• Mentor data engineers and review pipeline designs 
• Collaborate with analytics, AI, and business teams 
• Optimize data performance, cost, and reliability 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• SQL, Python, Spark 
• Data warehousing (BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift) 
• Streaming platforms (Kafka, Kinesis) 
• Cloud data services and orchestration tools 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–14 years of data engineering experience 
• Leadership in large-scale data platforms
