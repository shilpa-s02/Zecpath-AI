78. E-Commerce Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The E-Commerce Technical Lead drives development of high-performance, scalable online 
commerce platforms handling traffic, payments, and order fulfillment. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead architecture for e-commerce platforms 
• Optimize performance for high-traffic systems 
• Integrate payment gateways, logistics, and ERP 
• Implement security and fraud prevention 
• Support personalization and analytics features 
Required Skills 
• E-commerce platforms (Magento, Shopify, custom) 
• Java / Node.js / PHP 
• Payment gateways & APIs 
• Microservices and caching 
• Cloud scalability & DevOps 
Experience 
• 7–12 years in software engineering 
• 3+ years leading e-commerce platforms
