83. Project Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Project Technical Lead is responsible for technical execution of a specific project, 
ensuring architecture, delivery timelines, and quality align with project goals. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Own technical planning and execution for assigned projects 
• Translate business requirements into technical solutions 
• Coordinate tasks across multiple engineers 
• Ensure adherence to project timelines and scope 
• Identify technical risks and mitigation strategies 
• Support project management activities 
Required Skills 
• System design and implementation 
• Project-based delivery experience 
• Risk management 
• Agile / Waterfall methodologies 
• Stakeholder coordination 
Experience 
• 7–12 years in engineering 
• 3+ years leading project-based delivery
