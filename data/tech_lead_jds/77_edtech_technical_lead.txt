77. EdTech Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The EdTech Technical Lead leads development of scalable learning platforms, digital 
classrooms, assessment systems, and AI-driven education tools. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Architect LMS and learning platforms 
• Lead backend, frontend, and mobile teams 
• Implement content delivery and assessment systems 
• Integrate video streaming, analytics, and AI tools 
• Ensure platform scalability during peak usage 
Required Skills 
• LMS platforms, content delivery systems 
• JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python 
• Cloud scaling & CDN 
• Data analytics & AI integration 
• Agile product development 
Experience 
• 6–10 years in software development 
• 2+ years in EdTech or platform leadership
