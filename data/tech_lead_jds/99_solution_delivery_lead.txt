99. Solution Delivery Lead 
Role Overview 
The Solution Delivery Lead owns end-to-end technical delivery of client solutions, ensuring 
alignment between solution design, execution, and business outcomes. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead solution architecture and delivery execution 
• Coordinate cross-functional teams and vendors 
• Ensure solutions meet functional and non-functional requirements 
• Manage delivery risks, dependencies, and escalations 
• Track delivery metrics and client satisfaction 
• Support solution rollout and post-delivery stabilization 
Required Skills 
• Solution architecture and delivery management 
• Client engagement and communication 
• Integration and deployment expertise 
• Agile and hybrid delivery models 
• Strong problem-solving skills 
Engagement Type 
• Project-based / Client-facing 
Experience 
• 8–14 years in technical delivery 
• 4+ years in lead or solution roles
