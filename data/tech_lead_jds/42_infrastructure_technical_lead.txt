42. Infrastructure Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Infrastructure Technical Lead oversees compute, storage, networking, and hybrid 
infrastructure environments. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and manage on-prem and cloud infrastructure 
• Ensure system availability, capacity, and performance 
• Lead infrastructure automation and monitoring 
• Coordinate with security and operations teams 
• Manage upgrades, DR, and capacity planning 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Servers, networking, storage systems 
• Virtualization and cloud infrastructure 
• Automation and monitoring tools 
• Infrastructure security best practices 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–15 years of infrastructure experience 
• Leadership in enterprise infrastructure operations
