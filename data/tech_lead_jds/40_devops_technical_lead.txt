40. DevOps Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The DevOps Technical Lead drives automation, CI/CD, and DevOps culture to enable fast, 
reliable, and secure software delivery. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and manage CI/CD pipelines 
• Automate infrastructure and application deployments 
• Lead DevOps best practices and tooling standards 
• Integrate security into pipelines (DevSecOps) 
• Improve release reliability and deployment frequency 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• CI/CD tools (Jenkins, GitHub Actions, GitLab) 
• Infrastructure as Code 
• Containers and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes) 
• Monitoring and logging platforms 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–12 years of experience 
• Strong background in automation and DevOps
