74. CRM Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The CRM Technical Lead leads the design and development of CRM systems that support 
sales, marketing, and customer service operations. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design CRM technical architecture 
• Customize CRM workflows and automations 
• Manage integrations with ERP, analytics, and marketing tools 
• Ensure data integrity and security 
• Mentor CRM developers and administrators 
Required Skills 
• CRM platforms (Salesforce, Dynamics, Zoho, HubSpot) 
• API integrations 
• Automation and workflow tools 
• SQL and data modeling 
• Security best practices 
Experience 
• 6–10 years in CRM development 
• 3+ years in leadership roles
