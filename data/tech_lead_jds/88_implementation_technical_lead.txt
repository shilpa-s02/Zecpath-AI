88. Implementation Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Implementation Technical Lead manages technical implementation, deployment, and 
integration of enterprise solutions into customer environments. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead solution implementation and rollout 
• Manage environment setup and configuration 
• Oversee data migration and system integration 
• Ensure smooth go-live and post-deployment support 
• Coordinate with customer IT and operations teams 
• Document implementation processes and best practices 
Required Skills 
• Deployment and integration experience 
• Cloud and on-prem environments 
• CI/CD and release management 
• Troubleshooting and root-cause analysis 
• Customer collaboration 
Experience 
• 6–10 years in implementation or delivery roles 
• 3+ years in technical leadership 
Senior, Principal & Head-Level Technical Leads
