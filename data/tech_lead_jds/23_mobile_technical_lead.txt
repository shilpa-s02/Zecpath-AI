23. Mobile Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Mobile Technical Lead oversees end-to-end mobile application development across 
platforms, ensuring performance, security, and scalability. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead mobile architecture and platform decisions 
• Coordinate Android and iOS development teams 
• Define mobile coding standards and best practices 
• Optimize app performance and security 
• Manage app releases and store deployments 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Mobile app architecture 
• Android & iOS ecosystems 
• REST APIs, CI/CD for mobile 
• App security and performance optimization 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–12 years of mobile development experience 
• Experience leading cross-platform teams
