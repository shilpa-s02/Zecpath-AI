55. Test Automation Lead 
Role Overview 
The Test Automation Lead drives automation strategy, framework development, and test 
coverage to enable fast and reliable software delivery. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and maintain test automation frameworks 
• Define automation standards and best practices 
• Integrate automated tests into CI/CD pipelines 
• Analyze automation ROI and test coverage 
• Guide teams on automation tools and patterns 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Selenium, Playwright, Cypress 
• API and backend automation 
• CI/CD tools and pipelines 
• Programming/scripting languages 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–11 years of testing experience 
• Strong background in test automation
