49. GenAI Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The GenAI Technical Lead leads development of generative AI systems such as LLM-based 
applications, copilots, and autonomous agents. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design GenAI architectures using LLMs 
• Implement prompt engineering and RAG pipelines 
• Ensure security, privacy, and hallucination mitigation 
• Lead GenAI deployment, monitoring, and optimization 
• Collaborate with product, legal, and compliance teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• LLMs (OpenAI, open-source models) 
• Prompt engineering and vector databases 
• RAG, embeddings, model evaluation 
• AI safety and responsible AI practices 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–12 years of experience 
• Hands-on leadership in GenAI production systems
