65. IAM Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The IAM Technical Lead designs and manages identity and access management solutions to 
ensure secure and compliant access across systems. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Architect IAM solutions and access models 
• Implement authentication, authorization, and SSO 
• Manage user lifecycle and privileged access 
• Ensure compliance with access policies 
• Integrate IAM across cloud and enterprise systems 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• IAM platforms and protocols (OAuth, OIDC, SAML) 
• Privileged access management (PAM) 
• Directory services and identity federation 
• Zero Trust security models 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–14 years of IAM experience 
• Leadership in enterprise IAM implementations
