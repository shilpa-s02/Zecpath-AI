73. Enterprise Systems Lead 
Role Overview 
The Enterprise Systems Lead oversees large-scale enterprise applications ensuring reliability, 
integration, and alignment with business strategy. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead enterprise application architecture 
• Manage cross-platform system integrations 
• Coordinate with IT, security, and business teams 
• Ensure compliance, scalability, and availability 
• Drive modernization and digital transformation initiatives 
Required Skills 
• Enterprise architecture frameworks 
• Integration platforms & middleware 
• Cloud and on-prem hybrid systems 
• IT governance and compliance 
• Stakeholder communication 
Experience 
• 10–15 years in enterprise IT 
• 5+ years leading enterprise platforms
