68. ERP Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The ERP Technical Lead is responsible for leading the technical design, development, 
integration, and optimization of enterprise ERP systems to support core business processes. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead ERP system architecture and customization 
• Oversee module development, integrations, and data migration 
• Coordinate with functional consultants and business stakeholders 
• Ensure system performance, scalability, and security 
• Manage ERP upgrades, patches, and enhancements 
• Mentor ERP developers and technical consultants 
Required Skills 
• ERP platforms (SAP, Oracle, Dynamics, NetSuite) 
• SQL, PL/SQL, ERP scripting 
• System integration (APIs, middleware) 
• Performance tuning & troubleshooting 
• SDLC & Agile methodologies 
Experience 
• 7–12 years in ERP development 
• 3+ years in technical leadership roles
