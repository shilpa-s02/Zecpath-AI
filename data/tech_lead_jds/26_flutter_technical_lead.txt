26. Flutter Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Flutter Technical Lead leads cross-platform mobile development using Flutter, ensuring 
high performance and reusable codebases. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Architect Flutter applications for Android and iOS 
• Guide teams on Dart and Flutter widgets 
• Ensure performance and native integrations 
• Manage app testing and deployment 
• Mentor cross-platform developers 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Flutter, Dart 
• State management (Provider, Bloc, Riverpod) 
• REST APIs, platform channels 
• CI/CD and app publishing 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 6–10 years of mobile development experience 
• Strong Flutter production experience
