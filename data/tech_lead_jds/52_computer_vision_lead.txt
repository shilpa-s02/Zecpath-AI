52. Computer Vision Lead 
Role Overview 
The Computer Vision Lead leads development of vision-based AI systems for image and 
video analysis. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and deploy computer vision models 
• Lead image/video processing pipelines 
• Optimize models for accuracy and performance 
• Manage datasets, labeling, and evaluation 
• Collaborate with product and hardware teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• OpenCV, CNNs, vision transformers 
• TensorFlow, PyTorch 
• Image/video processing techniques 
• GPU acceleration and optimization 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–13 years of CV experience 
• Leadership in production vision systems
