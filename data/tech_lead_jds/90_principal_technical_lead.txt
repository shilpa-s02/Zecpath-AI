90. Principal Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Principal Technical Lead provides cross-team technical leadership, defining architecture 
direction and influencing long-term technology strategy. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Own architecture across multiple products or platforms 
• Define technical vision, standards, and best practices 
• Lead design reviews and architectural governance 
• Drive innovation and adoption of new technologies 
• Mentor senior engineers and technical leads 
• Partner with leadership on technical strategy 
Required Skills 
• Distributed systems and large-scale architecture 
• Technology evaluation and roadmap planning 
• Cross-team technical influence 
• Performance, security, and scalability expertise 
• Executive-level communication 
Experience 
• 12–18 years in engineering 
• 5+ years in senior/principal roles
