102. AI Platform Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The AI Platform Technical Lead builds and governs scalable platforms that enable data 
science and engineering teams to develop, deploy, and operate AI solutions efficiently. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design AI/ML platform architecture 
• Enable model training, deployment, and monitoring 
• Standardize MLOps and GenAI workflows 
• Ensure scalability, reliability, and cost efficiency 
• Collaborate with data science and product teams 
• Define AI platform standards and best practices 
Required Skills 
• ML platforms and pipelines 
• MLOps and model lifecycle management 
• Cloud AI services 
• Kubernetes and workflow orchestration 
• Platform engineering 
Experience 
• 8–14 years in data or platform engineering 
• 3+ years leading AI platforms
