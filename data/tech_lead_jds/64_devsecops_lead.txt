64. DevSecOps Lead 
Role Overview 
The DevSecOps Lead integrates security into CI/CD pipelines and development workflows, 
enabling secure and rapid software delivery. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Embed security tools into CI/CD pipelines 
• Automate security testing and policy enforcement 
• Promote shift-left security practices 
• Collaborate with DevOps and development teams 
• Monitor and improve security posture continuously 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• CI/CD pipelines and automation 
• SAST, DAST, container and dependency scanning 
• Cloud-native security tools 
• DevSecOps practices 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–13 years of experience 
• Background in DevOps or security engineering
