82. Technical Team Lead 
Role Overview 
The Technical Team Lead manages a team of engineers while remaining hands-on with 
design, coding, and technical decision-making to ensure high-quality delivery. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead day-to-day technical activities of the team 
• Review code, enforce best practices, and maintain quality standards 
• Break down requirements into technical tasks 
• Mentor and guide engineers 
• Collaborate with product managers and architects 
• Track sprint progress and resolve technical blockers 
Required Skills 
• Strong hands-on development experience 
• Team leadership and mentoring 
• Agile/Scrum methodologies 
• Code review and design skills 
• Excellent communication 
Experience 
• 6–10 years in software development 
• 2+ years leading engineering teams
