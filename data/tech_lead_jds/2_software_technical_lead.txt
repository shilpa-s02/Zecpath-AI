2. Software Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Software Technical Lead oversees end-to-end software development, ensuring design 
quality, maintainability, and alignment with business goals. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Own software architecture and technical roadmap 
• Lead development activities across backend, frontend, and integrations 
• Drive coding standards, testing strategies, and documentation 
• Coordinate with QA, DevOps, and product teams 
• Ensure secure, scalable, and high-performance software delivery 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Expertise in software engineering principles and design patterns 
• Experience with modern frameworks and cloud-native development 
• CI/CD, automated testing, and version control 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–12 years of experience in software development 
• Proven experience as a senior engineer or technical lead
