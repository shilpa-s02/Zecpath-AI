37. AWS Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The AWS Technical Lead owns architecture and delivery of cloud solutions on AWS, 
ensuring scalability, security, and operational excellence. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design AWS-based architectures and services 
• Lead teams on EC2, EKS, Lambda, RDS, S3, VPC 
• Implement security, networking, and IAM best practices 
• Optimize cost using AWS Well-Architected Framework 
• Review cloud designs and production workloads 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• AWS core services and architecture patterns 
• Terraform or CloudFormation 
• CI/CD pipelines and container platforms 
• Cloud monitoring and logging 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–13 years of experience 
• AWS certifications (preferred)
