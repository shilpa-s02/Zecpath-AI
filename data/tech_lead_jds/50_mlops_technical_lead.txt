50. MLOps Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The MLOps Technical Lead ensures reliable deployment, monitoring, and scaling of machine 
learning models in production environments. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design ML CI/CD and automation pipelines 
• Manage model versioning, monitoring, and retraining 
• Ensure reproducibility and governance 
• Collaborate with DevOps, data, and ML teams 
• Improve ML system reliability and performance 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• ML pipelines and orchestration tools 
• Kubernetes, Docker, CI/CD 
• Model monitoring and drift detection 
• Cloud ML platforms 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–14 years of experience 
• Strong background in MLOps or DevOps for ML
