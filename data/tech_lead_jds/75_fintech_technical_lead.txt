75. FinTech Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The FinTech Technical Lead leads the design and development of secure, scalable financial 
technology systems including payments, digital banking, lending, and trading platforms. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Architect and lead FinTech platform development 
• Ensure compliance with financial regulations and security standards 
• Design high-availability and low-latency systems 
• Integrate payment gateways, banking APIs, and third-party services 
• Lead code reviews, technical decisions, and performance optimization 
• Collaborate with compliance, product, and risk teams 
Required Skills 
• Payments, wallets, lending, or trading systems 
• Java / Python / Node.js 
• Microservices, event-driven systems 
• PCI-DSS, SOX, GDPR 
• Cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP) 
Experience 
• 7–12 years in software engineering 
• 3+ years in FinTech or financial systems leadership
