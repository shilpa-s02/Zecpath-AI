94. Head of Technology 
Role Overview 
The Head of Technology defines the organization’s overall technology strategy, ensuring 
platforms, teams, and investments support business growth. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define and own technology vision and roadmap 
• Oversee architecture, platforms, and engineering practices 
• Align technology initiatives with business goals 
• Drive innovation, security, and scalability 
• Build and mentor senior technical leadership 
• Manage technology risks and governance 
Required Skills 
• Technology strategy and leadership 
• Enterprise architecture knowledge 
• Budgeting and resource planning 
• Executive stakeholder communication 
• Risk, security, and compliance management 
Experience 
• 15–22 years in technology 
• 7+ years in head/director-level roles
