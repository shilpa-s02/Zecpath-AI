97. Contract Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Contract Technical Lead is engaged for a fixed duration to lead technical delivery, 
architecture, and quality for specific programs or initiatives. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Own technical direction for contract engagements 
• Lead development teams and technical planning 
• Ensure quality, performance, and security standards 
• Coordinate with delivery managers and clients 
• Manage technical risks and dependencies 
• Support onboarding and handover at contract end 
Required Skills 
• Full-stack or platform expertise 
• Delivery and execution focus 
• Team leadership under timelines 
• Cloud, DevOps, and CI/CD knowledge 
• Strong stakeholder management 
Engagement Type 
• Fixed-term contract / Onsite / Remote 
Experience 
• 7–12 years in engineering 
• 3+ years in lead or contract roles
