92. Lead Architect 
Role Overview 
The Lead Architect is responsible for defining and governing system architecture, ensuring 
scalability, security, and alignment with business goals. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design end-to-end system architectures 
• Define architectural standards and principles 
• Review and approve technical designs 
• Guide teams on architecture implementation 
• Evaluate new tools, platforms, and frameworks 
• Ensure systems meet performance and security standards 
Required Skills 
• Enterprise and solution architecture 
• Cloud and hybrid architectures 
• Integration patterns and data design 
• Security and compliance awareness 
• Technical documentation and governance 
Experience 
• 12–18 years in software architecture 
• 5+ years in architect or lead roles
