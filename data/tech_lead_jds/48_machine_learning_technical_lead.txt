48. Machine Learning Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Machine Learning Technical Lead oversees end-to-end ML systems, from feature 
engineering to production deployment and monitoring. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design ML architectures and feature pipelines 
• Guide model selection, training, and evaluation 
• Ensure scalability, performance, and explainability 
• Lead model deployment and monitoring strategies 
• Mentor ML engineers and data scientists 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• ML algorithms and evaluation metrics 
• Python, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch 
• Feature stores and model monitoring tools 
• Cloud-based ML platforms 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 7–13 years of ML experience 
• Proven leadership in production ML systems
