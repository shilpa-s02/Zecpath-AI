7. Lead Systems Engineer 
Role Overview 
The Lead Systems Engineer oversees system-level design, integration, and reliability of 
large-scale platforms and infrastructure. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and maintain system architecture and integrations 
• Ensure system availability, reliability, and performance 
• Lead system troubleshooting and root cause analysis 
• Collaborate with security and infrastructure teams 
• Document system processes and standards 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Deep understanding of system architecture and OS concepts 
• Networking, storage, and infrastructure expertise 
• Automation and monitoring tools 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–14 years of systems engineering experience 
• Experience leading mission-critical systems
