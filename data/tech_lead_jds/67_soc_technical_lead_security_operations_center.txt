67. SOC Technical Lead (Security Operations Center) 
Role Overview 
The SOC Technical Lead leads security monitoring, detection, and incident response 
operations. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Oversee SOC tools, processes, and analysts 
• Lead incident detection, response, and forensics 
• Improve threat intelligence and detection capabilities 
• Ensure 24/7 security monitoring and SLA compliance 
• Coordinate response to major security incidents 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• SIEM and SOAR platforms 
• Threat detection and incident response 
• Log analysis and forensics tools 
• Security operations best practices 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–14 years of security operations experience 
• Leadership in SOC or incident response teams
