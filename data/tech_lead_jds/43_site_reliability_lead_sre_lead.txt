43. Site Reliability Lead (SRE Lead) 
Role Overview 
The SRE Lead ensures system reliability, availability, and performance using engineering 
principles and automation. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define SRE strategy, SLIs, SLOs, and error budgets 
• Lead incident management and root cause analysis 
• Automate operations and reliability practices 
• Improve observability and system resilience 
• Collaborate with development and platform teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Reliability engineering and automation 
• Monitoring, logging, and alerting tools 
• Kubernetes and cloud platforms 
• Incident response and postmortems 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–14 years of experience 
• Strong background in SRE or DevOps
