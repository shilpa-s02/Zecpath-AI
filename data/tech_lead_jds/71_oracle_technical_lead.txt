71. Oracle Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Oracle Technical Lead manages development and integration of Oracle enterprise 
systems including databases, middleware, and Oracle ERP solutions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead Oracle ERP and database architecture 
• Develop PL/SQL packages, triggers, and reports 
• Manage integrations and data pipelines 
• Optimize performance and ensure system reliability 
• Oversee upgrades and system enhancements 
Required Skills 
• Oracle ERP / EBS / Fusion 
• PL/SQL, SQL, Oracle Forms & Reports 
• Oracle Integration Cloud 
• Performance tuning 
• Security and compliance best practices 
Experience 
• 7–12 years with Oracle technologies 
• 3+ years leading technical teams
