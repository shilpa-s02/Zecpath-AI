106. Responsible AI Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Responsible AI Technical Lead ensures ethical, transparent, and compliant design and 
deployment of AI and GenAI systems. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define responsible AI frameworks and practices 
• Ensure fairness, explainability, and bias mitigation 
• Implement AI governance and auditability 
• Collaborate with legal, compliance, and ethics teams 
• Monitor AI systems for unintended outcomes 
• Guide teams on ethical AI design 
Required Skills 
• AI ethics and governance 
• Model explainability and bias detection 
• Regulatory compliance for AI systems 
• Data privacy and security 
• Cross-functional collaboration 
Experience 
• 8–14 years in AI or data systems 
• 3+ years in AI governance or leadership roles
