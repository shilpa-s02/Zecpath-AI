79. Telecom Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Telecom Technical Lead oversees development of telecom platforms supporting billing, 
OSS/BSS, network management, and customer services. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design telecom system architectures 
• Lead development of OSS/BSS platforms 
• Integrate network APIs and legacy systems 
• Ensure high availability and fault tolerance 
• Collaborate with network and operations teams 
Required Skills 
• Telecom OSS/BSS systems 
• Java / C++ / Python 
• Distributed systems 
• Messaging & event streaming 
• Telecom protocols & APIs 
Experience 
• 8–14 years in telecom systems 
• 4+ years in technical leadership roles
