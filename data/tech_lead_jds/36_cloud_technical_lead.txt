36. Cloud Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Cloud Technical Lead is responsible for defining and leading cloud architecture, 
migration, and optimization strategies across applications and infrastructure. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and govern cloud architecture across platforms 
• Lead cloud migration and modernization initiatives 
• Define cloud security, networking, and cost-optimization standards 
• Collaborate with development, DevOps, and security teams 
• Ensure high availability, scalability, and disaster recovery 
• Mentor cloud engineers and review designs 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Public cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP) 
• Cloud networking, IAM, and security best practices 
• Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, ARM, CloudFormation) 
• Monitoring and cost management tools 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 8–14 years of IT experience 
• 3+ years in cloud architecture or technical leadership
