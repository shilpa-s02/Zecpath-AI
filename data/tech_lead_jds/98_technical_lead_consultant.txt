98. Technical Lead Consultant 
Role Overview 
The Technical Lead Consultant advises organizations on architecture, technology strategy, 
and execution while guiding teams through transformation or complex delivery. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Assess existing systems and technical maturity 
• Propose architecture improvements and roadmaps 
• Lead technical workshops and design sessions 
• Support engineering teams during execution 
• Provide risk analysis and mitigation strategies 
• Act as trusted technical advisor to stakeholders 
Required Skills 
• Architecture and system design expertise 
• Consulting and advisory mindset 
• Technology evaluation and modernization 
• Executive communication 
• Documentation and presentation skills 
Engagement Type 
• Consulting / Advisory / Hybrid 
Experience 
• 10–18 years in engineering 
• Prior consulting or advisory experience preferred
