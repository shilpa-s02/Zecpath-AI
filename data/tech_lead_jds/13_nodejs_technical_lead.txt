13. Node.js Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Node.js Technical Lead leads high-performance backend services and APIs using 
JavaScript/TypeScript, focusing on scalability and real-time systems. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and build scalable Node.js backend services 
• Lead development using Express, NestJS, or similar frameworks 
• Implement REST and GraphQL APIs 
• Ensure performance, security, and reliability 
• Mentor team members and review code 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Node.js, JavaScript/TypeScript 
• Express.js, NestJS 
• MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Redis 
• Microservices, Docker, Kubernetes 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 6–10 years of backend development experience 
• Strong leadership in JavaScript-based ecosystems
