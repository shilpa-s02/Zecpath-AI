28. Technical Architecture Lead 
Role Overview 
The Technical Architecture Lead owns the overall technical architecture of products and 
platforms, ensuring scalability, security, performance, and alignment with business strategy. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Define end-to-end system architecture and technical standards 
• Evaluate and select technologies, frameworks, and platforms 
• Guide development teams on architectural patterns and best practices 
• Ensure non-functional requirements (scalability, availability, security) 
• Review and approve high-level design documents 
• Manage technical debt and long-term architecture roadmap 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• System architecture and design patterns 
• Distributed systems and cloud architecture 
• Microservices, APIs, and messaging systems 
• Security, performance, and scalability principles 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 10–15 years of software engineering experience 
• 3+ years in architecture or technical leadership roles
