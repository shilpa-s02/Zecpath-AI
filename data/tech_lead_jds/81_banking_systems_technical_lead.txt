81. Banking Systems Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Banking Systems Technical Lead leads development and modernization of core banking, 
digital banking, and financial transaction systems. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead architecture of core banking platforms 
• Ensure transaction consistency and security 
• Integrate regulatory and reporting systems 
• Modernize legacy banking applications 
• Collaborate with compliance, audit, and business teams 
Required Skills 
• Core banking systems 
• Java / COBOL / .NET 
• High-availability transaction systems 
• Security and compliance standards 
• API and integration platforms 
Experience 
• 8–15 years in banking IT 
• 4+ years as a technical lead 
Delivery, Team & Project-Oriented Technical Leads
