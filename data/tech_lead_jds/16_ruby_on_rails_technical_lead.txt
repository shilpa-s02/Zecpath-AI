16. Ruby on Rails Technical Lead 
Role Overview 
The Ruby on Rails Technical Lead leads rapid application development while maintaining 
clean architecture, performance, and scalability. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead Rails application design and development 
• Ensure MVC best practices and clean code 
• Optimize performance and database queries 
• Mentor developers and conduct code reviews 
• Collaborate with product and UX teams 
Required Skills & Technologies 
• Ruby, Ruby on Rails 
• ActiveRecord, REST APIs 
• PostgreSQL/MySQL 
• CI/CD and cloud deployment 
Experience & Qualifications 
• 6–10 years of Ruby on Rails experience 
• Experience leading SaaS or product teams
